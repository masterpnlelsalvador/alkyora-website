# Alkyora Brand Notes

## Positioning

Alkyora is an AI Systems Studio with Security Built In.

The brand helps businesses design, launch, audit, and harden AI-powered websites, automations, workflows, and business systems with practical security-aware implementation from day one.

## Tone of Voice

- Clear
- Premium
- Technical but understandable
- Calm and confident
- Business-focused
- Security-aware without fearmongering
- Practical rather than hype-driven

## Color Palette

- Background: `#050816`
- Surface: `#0B1020`
- Primary: `#7C3AED`
- Accent: `#22D3EE`
- Text: `#F8FAFC`
- Muted text: `#94A3B8`
- Security/success accent: `#10B981`

## Typography

- Geist Sans for interface and editorial copy
- Geist Mono for labels, badges, system language, and small technical details

## Messaging Pillars

- AI systems built fast and secured from day one
- Move fast without breaking trust
- From AI experiments to secure business systems
- Security-aware implementation as a foundation, not an afterthought
- Practical reviews, documentation, guardrails, and safer data handling

## Terms to Use

- AI Systems Studio
- security-aware
- practical review
- reduce risk
- identify weak points
- improve data handling
- workflow guardrails
- launch readiness
- business-ready infrastructure
- system review

## Terms to Avoid

- 100% secure
- guaranteed protection
- fully compliant
- hack-proof
- military-grade security
- revolutionize your business
- cutting-edge AI
- generic AI agency claims

## Disclaimer Pattern

Alkyora should be clear that it does not replace formal legal compliance work, enterprise cybersecurity teams, or formal penetration testing when those are required.
